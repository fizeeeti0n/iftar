About the Event

A Sacred Evening of Togetherness

As the blessed month of Ramadan graces us once more, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at UAP invites all students, alumni, and faculty to gather in the spirit of community, gratitude, and shared blessings.

The Iftar Mahfil 2026 is more than a meal â€” it is a celebration of bonds forged in knowledge and faith. We break our fast together, reflect on our journey, and strengthen the ties that make our community extraordinary.

Join us at UAP Plaza for an evening of Quran recitation, heartfelt du'a, joyous networking, and a delicious Iftar feast as the sun sets on the horizon.

Spiritual Gathering

Begin the evening with Quran recitation and communal du'a, nurturing the soul and lifting hearts together.

Community Networking

Connect with students, alumni, and faculty in a warm and welcoming atmosphere that celebrates unity.

Delicious Iftar Dinner

Enjoy a beautifully curated Iftar spread, bringing the joy of breaking fast with loved ones and colleagues.
